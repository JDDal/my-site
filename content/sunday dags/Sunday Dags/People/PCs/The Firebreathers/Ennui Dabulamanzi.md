Iados "Ennui" Dabulamanzi (male Tiefling warlock) is a librarian working for the [[Library of the Tattered Page]] alongside his mother [[Kali Dabulamanzi]] (human). Six years ago, his half brother [[Gregory Dabulamanzi]] and half sister [[Alma Dabulamanzi]] (both human) left the library to become adventurers after the death of their stepfather (and Ennui's tiefling father) [[Ezra Dabulamanzi]]. He was sent his sister's axe the [[Winter's Bite]] 2 months ago under mysterious circumstances

  
1 Why are you adventuring: Experimenting with his newfound powers after receiving the famed axe; and hoping to track down his sister [[Alma Dabulamanzi]] and brother [[Gregory Dabulamanzi]]

2 What motivates you: Knowledge! The central tenet of the [[Library of the Tattered Page]] is that all knowledge should be shared with everyone, all the time

3 Where did you grow up: [[Caldera]], in the poorer areas

4 Why are you your current class: He was gifted mysterious eldritch powers when he received his axe [[Winter's Bite]] (formerly belonged to his sister [[Alma Dabulamanzi]])

5 when have you failed: Ennui was content to stay in the library reading obscure books after his father passed, and failed at several odd jobs. with no breadwinner, his siblings took to adventuring and sending home gold to support the family/Library
