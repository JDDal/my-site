 Torg Lighteyes (Half Orc, Barbarian, Male)

6' 5", grey-white hair, white-blue eyes (no pupils, etc)

Torg comes from the [[Del Empire]] of coastal piratey raiders. 

Raised by the tribe, as is custom (doesnt know parents). Torg was a loyal deckhand to [[Captain Brem]] (human pirate, male, now deceased) on the ship '[[The Barnacle]].' 

When faced with huge enemy fleet, Brem ordered 'The Barnacle' to flee instead of face a glorious death. The Empire managed to achieve a draw in the battle, and Brem was put to death. Unbeknownsted to Torg, Brem had actually betrayed the Empire

garb is weathered. Tattoo of sea birds' wings across his upper back. also has a horn and taiko-style drum stick on his belt

Was tasked by the Empire with taking [[Brem's ashes]] to be scattered in a vast desert (Brem disgraced the Empire and must be laid to rest as far from the sea as possible to atone)

Sees ancestral pirate-barbarian spirits from time to time. Custom dictates that Torg was set to captain his own ship (in due time) because of his ability to commune with ancestors (Path of the Ancestral Guardian once we hit level 3)

- describes his crewmates as 'cousin ____'. so he's always mentioning different cousins

- he's superstitious, like most pirates - for instance 'if an elf looks in your eyes, they're trying to read your thoughts'

- remembers his first conquest - a merchant vessel called '[[The Laden Goose]]' - it was glorious
