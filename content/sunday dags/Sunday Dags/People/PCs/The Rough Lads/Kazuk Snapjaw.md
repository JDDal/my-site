Kaszuk Snapjaw / Druid / Lizardfolk / Firstborn son of [[Varak Snapjaw]], head Lizardfolk Tribesman

  
**Step 1**: Description:

Kaszuk is a 17 year old Lizardfolk Druid (10 years marks adulthood for Lizardfolk). He stands at 6'8" covered head to toe in green and blue scales. A colorful frill sits atop his head and runs down the back of his skull that makes his full form over 7' tall. He wears hide armor/clothing he crafts himself for the benefit of civilized society but feels equally or more comfortable in his own scales. He is the firstborn son of the head of his tribe. He is championed in his tribe for his connection to the natural arcane elements and proficiency hunting

  
**Step 2**: Goals:  
Kaszuk is the first of his tribe to ever leave their sacred hunting grounds. An elder tribe shaman divined a [[three-part prophecy]] that changed the course of Kasuk's life forever:

1. The tribe's native lands will be destroyed by a supernatural, otherworldly force.
    
2. The only intervention from this destruction is for Kaszuk, firstborn of the head of the tribe, to leave his native lands in the pursuit of higher knowledge in the natural and unnatural world.
    
3. This quest for knowledge and experience seems centered somehow around unknown persons in [[Caldera]] and surrounding areas
    

  

**Step 3**: Secrets:

1. Lizardfolk keep to themselves and make no distinction between beasts/mankind/monsters. On the exterior Kaszuk is no different, but he does harbor a passing curiosity in society and it's many machinations. He wonders if this feeling he has kept secret is at all to do with his “chosenness” in the prophecy.
    
2. One of the player characters he meets in Caldera will be key to his quest, though neither know it yet.
    

  
**Step 4**: Relations:

1. [[Varak Snapjaw]] – Father and Tribe Leader
    
2. [[Drazda Glimmerscale]] – Elder Shaman, Diviner of Prophecy
    
3. [[Grauk Sharpclaw]] (Enemy) – Lead hunter from neighboring rival tribe
    

  
**Step 5:** **Flavor facts:**

1. **Kaszuk is omnivorous, but seriously prefers meat and will eat it exclusively if available**
    
2. **He carries small “toothpicks” he fashioned from the bones of beasts he's hunted and regularly picks his teeth during idle time**
    
3. **Still very unfamiliar with the niceties required in society he may glower in silence when asked a question he doesn't care for or find interesting/necessary.**
