Dr. Andross Wold, Half-Elf, Sorcerer (dagron blood), Archaeologist, Professor of Draconic Lore at [[The Caldera Institute of the Arcane]]

1: Description: Middle aged (A fit and healthy 65), unkempt black hair with grey/white streaks, wearing a long black petti-coat with fancy gold buttons, Has a tattered backpack stuffed full of archaeologist tools (pick-axe, chalk, rope, shovel, brushes, etc,), Has a noticeable limp to his walk from a spelunking accident, piercing golden eyes and frown lines, carries a large tome with a bust of a golden dragon upon the cover (Strapped to his hip)

2: Goals: Hopes to one day be renowned for his large contribution to Draconic scholarship (IT BELONGS IN A MUSEUM!!!)
