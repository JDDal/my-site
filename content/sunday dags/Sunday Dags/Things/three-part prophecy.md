a prophecy by [[Drazda Glimmerscale]] foretold to [[Kazuk Snapjaw]] concerning the fate of his tribe


1. The tribe's native lands will be destroyed by a supernatural, otherworldly force.
    
2. The only intervention from this destruction is for Kaszuk, firstborn of the head of the tribe, to leave his native lands in the pursuit of higher knowledge in the natural and unnatural world.
    
3. This quest for knowledge and experience seems centered somehow around unknown persons in [[Caldera]] and surrounding areas
