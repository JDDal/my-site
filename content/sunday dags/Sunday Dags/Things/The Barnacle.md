The Barnacle was a pirate vessel belonging to the [[Del Empire]]

it was captained by [[Captain Brem]]
[[Torg Lighteyes]] was a deckhand on board

When faced with huge enemy fleet, Brem ordered 'The Barnacle' to flee instead of face a glorious death. The Empire managed to achieve a draw in the battle, and Brem was put to death. 

Brem had actually betrayed the Empire
