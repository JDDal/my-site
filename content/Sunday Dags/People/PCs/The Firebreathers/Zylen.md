Zylen hails from the shattered red dragonborn tribes of the [[Varelian Crags]], found far to the north of [[Caldera]]. The once proud dragonborn tribes that were lauded in legend and feared by all, have long declined into itinerant bands that roam the alpine valleys of the Crags. Zylen’s tribe adheres to the [[Rite of the Magma Talon]]

Zylen joined the [[Boynton’s Bloody Blades]], a rough and tumble mercenary band that was well known within [[Caldera]]’s criminal underworld. Zylen served for five years as a capable and brutal soldier of the Blades, and was the only survivor of infamous [[Black Betty’s Pub Massacre]]. 

The aftermath of the massacre led to the disbanding of the Blades, which has caused Zylen to be left to his own devices. Without the structure of the chain of command within the Blades, or the ever watchful eye of his tribe’s elders, Zylen now spends his days availing himself of booze, coveting shiny objects, and “accidentally” burning down taverns after being involved in bar fights.
