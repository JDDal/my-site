Lovia Stronglogger Nulakukena (goliath cleric) was born and raised in the [[Nula Mountains]], a mountainous region to the east of [[Caldera]], that is known for being a major logging town. Her entire village worship [[Dama Elliza]] (Mother of Rains and Reaping, goddess of agriculture and farming, god of nature, weather) and she grants them their healing powers.

A few decades ago, several groups of halflings, dwarfs, and gnomes moved into the hills around Nula and Lovia's villagers found them very useful in helping dig up the tree roots from their logged trees. Once a year, the entire village would hold a logging competition known as the [[Strongest Logman]] and Lovia is a 3-time champion. 

Growing up, Lovia's parents are the main bartering force for the logs and so she knows a lot of different merchants and trade routes all throughout Caldera.

2 What motivates you: gaining life experience, making friends and protecting the little folk she cares so much for back in Nula.

3 Where did you grow up: Nula Mountains to the east of Caldera
