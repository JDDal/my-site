(human pirate, male, now deceased) was captain of the ship '[[The Barnacle]].' 

member of the [[Del Empire]]

When faced with huge enemy fleet, Brem ordered 'The Barnacle' to flee instead of face a glorious death. The Empire managed to achieve a draw in the battle, and Brem was put to death. 

Brem had actually betrayed the Empire

[[Torg Lighteyes]] was tasked with taking [[Brem's ashes]] to be scattered in a vast desert (Brem disgraced the Empire and must be laid to rest as far from the sea as possible to atone)
