Caldera is a large city in the North Western part of its continent. It is built in the bend of a large river and is about 50 miles from the coast. It is surrounded by thick forests and farmland has been carved out of the woods. Caldera is a huge melting pot that is home to every type of civilized creature and some that you wouldn't consider civilized like goblins.  There is no official ruler, there is a [[King of Caldera]] but as far as anyone knows the royal family was exiled years ago and no one misses them. There are two police forces in the city, the "[[Kings Watch]]" which is funded by the nobility (nobility is any family wealthy enough to claim to be) and the "[[City Watch]]" which is funded by [[the Mages guild]]. The Mages guild is a Continental (maybe worldwide) organization, that wields a huge amount of power.

Large City's and farms have "[[wardstones]]" that protect them from certain types of monsters.

Magic is very rare and expensive.

[[Alchemy]] is a cheaper form of magic that is much more common. 

There are a ton of Gods and Spirits, but the Gods that seem to call the shots are "[[the twelve]]" I wont list them here.
